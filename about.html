About Me

A Developer and Graphic Designer Who Specializes
In Backend Programming and Visual Design.

My name is Yusuf Maulana, and I am a passionate backend developer and graphic designer. I specialize in creating visually appealing designs and building efficient backend systems. Currently, I am studying Informatics at Universitas Horizon Indonesia.

As a backend developer, I work with technologies like PHP, Python, and Java, while as a graphic designer, I focus on creating stunning visuals for websites, branding, and digital media. I believe in the power of design and technology coming together to create seamless, user-friendly experiences.

Feel free to reach out if you're looking for a collaboration or advice on backend development or graphic design.

How I Work

Backend development involves the server-side logic, database management, and overall architecture that powers applications. As a backend developer, I ensure that the server, database, and application are seamlessly integrated and run efficiently.

Graphic design is the art of creating visual content to communicate messages. Using design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma, I craft logos, web designs, social media graphics, and more, making sure the visuals align with the message and the brand identity.

I believe that great design and effective backend systems go hand in hand. As both a backend developer and graphic designer, I aim to create websites and applications that are not only functional but also visually stunning, offering users an engaging and intuitive experience.
